SIGNALIS

a dream about dreaming

Wake Up

Wake up. Remember what you promised. Your ship has crashed on an unknown planet. Systems are down, beyond repair. As you assess the wreckage, you discover your partner—your pilot—is missing. So is one of the spacesuits. There's only one path left to take.

You leave the ship—Penrose 512, a scout shuttle crafted to carry humanity's emissaries into the dark stretches of alien stars. It was a cheap but dependable vessel. Now, it's ruined. You step out into the unknown, leaving the wreck behind.

The only greeting is the blizzard, howling around you, an endless storm that conceals the landscape. You can barely see two meters ahead. There's no sign of her—Alina Seo, wasn't it? Yes, that was her name.

Questions emerge, drifting towards you. But one of them overshadows all the rest: Who are you? Can you even answer that?

You're not human. You are a Replika unit, codename LSTR-512—a Kosmo-Pioneer specialist android. Your mission was simple: to maintain Penrose during its journey and assist your Gestalt pilot. But that mission is meaningless now. You push through the blizzard, until you find... a hole, burrowed into the frozen ground. An unsettling sight, for it's not just a hole—it's lined with red, raw flesh.

You descend, moving deeper into the unknown. The tunnel leads you to... a room. Strangely welcoming, almost cozy. An old radio rests on the table beside a worn, ancient-looking book—The King in Yellow. You pick it up, and the radio crackles to life, broadcasting garbled numbers in a distorted voice. Your vision wavers, and then you hear it—her voice, familiar yet haunting, whispering:

Great holes secretly are digged where earths pores ought to suffice and things have learnt to walk that ought to crawl.
Remember our promise
